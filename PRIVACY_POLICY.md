## Overview

Starmy ("the App") is an educational app for children.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping the app experience safe.

## Data Collection

**Starmy does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.**

The app stores learning-related information locally on your device (for example, progress and in-app settings) so your child can continue where they left off.
This data stays on your device.

We do not require account creation and we do not operate user data servers for this app.

## Third-Party Services

Starmy does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or profiling tools.

## Children's Privacy

Starmy is designed for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

## Data Security

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you control access to it through your device security settings (such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID).
